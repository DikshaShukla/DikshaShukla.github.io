Fake Information Research

Understanding the charateristics of Fake Information and developing methods for its early detection and spread.

  • Health Misinformation - Categorizing and detecting social media health misinformation content, understanding the network behavior for misinformation spreads, the motivations, and methods to measure and conduct effective interventions (defensive measures to limit the reach of misinformation)
  • News, Trust, and Information Quality - Modeling the effect of misinformation consumption on the person's attitude and interpretation of news quality, trust, and bias.
  • Affective Polarization, Conflict, and Violence - Understanding affective polarization due to spread of political, religious, ethnic, or linguistic misinformation over the social networks. Understanding the correlation between the online misinformation, dangerous speech and offline harm.
  • Digital Literacy, Demographics, and Misinformation
  • Cross-platform Misinformation Ecosystem
